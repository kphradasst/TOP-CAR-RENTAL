TOP CAR RENTAL

Customer Policy

  1. Security Deposit:
    Php15,000. Refundable after rental. Deductions apply for:
    • Fuel shortage (charges per quarter tank):
      • Van: Php1,200
      • SUV/Pick-up: Php1,000
      • MPV/Crossover: Php800
    • Unpaid tolls or dirty return: Php350 cleaning fee
  2. Insurance:
    Top Car Rental has separate insurance. Third-party sites like Carflexi or Economy Car Rental are not covered.
  3. Damage Liability:
    Renter covers all damage/loss costs unless valid documentation is provided.
  4. Excess Fee:
    Max Php7,500 per incident (vehicle-only). Optional 3rd-party insurance: Php300/day.
  5. Downtime Charges:
    Up to 7 days of rental cost applies during vehicle repairs.
  6. Claims Policy:
    All claims must be settled directly with your booking provider.
